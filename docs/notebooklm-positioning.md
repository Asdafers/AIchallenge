#### 2\. Methodological Rigor: Grounding Claims in Evidence

Judges will prioritize agents that demonstrate "relevant rigour." Methodic is not a "chat" interface; it is a system that "knows" how to conduct research by adhering to the standards of sociological and psychological methodology. Grounding our agents in Bandalos’s  **Measurement Theory**  is the differentiator that elevates Methodic from a "toy" to an enterprise-grade tool for  **Data Integrity** .

##### Methodological Grounding

Our multi-agent architecture is anchored in foundational evidence from the "Source Context":

* **Dillman’s "Tailored Design Method":**  Methodic adopts an integrative approach, using interactivity and the layout of visual/aural stimuli to achieve optimum response rates in digital environments.  
* **Robinson’s "Designing Quality Survey Questions":**  Our Question Design Agent crafts engaging, top-quality questions specifically designed to eliminate "survey apathy" and evoke in-depth insights.  
* **Saldaña’s "Coding Manual for Qualitative Researchers":**  Our Data Quality Agent performs real-time thematic coding, identifying patterns and understanding data for successful, effective analysis.

##### The Authenticity Principle & Ethical Boundary

We must avoid over-claiming that AI replaces human judgment. As highlighted by  **Johnson & Rasulova (University of Bath)** , qualitative research involves an "authenticity principle" where the human touch is vital for meaningful conclusions. We position Methodic as a  **"Peer Debriefer"**  (a concept supported by Delve). The agent highlights missed perspectives and streamlines tedious tasks, but the final interpretation remains a collaborative, value-based human effort. This grounding ensures  **measurement validity**  and increases our Business Case score.

#### 3\. Competitive Analysis: Defending the "Traceable Evidence" Layer

In the 2026 Qualitative Data Analysis (QDA) landscape, "Traceability" is our primary weapon against legacy software like NVivo and AI-native entrants like Delve or Sopact Sense.

##### The Methodic Advantage

1. **Sopact Sense vs. Methodic:**  Sopact Sense notes that  **80% of analysis time is wasted**  before coding begins. Methodic eliminates this bottleneck through real-time thematic coding and  **passage-to-code citations** . Every finding is linked to a direct "trail" from raw data to insight, allowing a funder to click a theme and see the exact transcript snippet that generated it.  
2. **Delve vs. Methodic:**  While Delve focuses on an intuitive interface for human coders, Methodic provides autonomous, consistent logic across thousands of transcripts that human judgment calls cannot match for scale.

##### Defensible Insights

* **Trait 1: Ambiguity Reduction:**  Methodic tracks "Ambiguity Reduction %"—a metric showing how many vague responses were clarified through active probing. This is our primary defense against the "Garbage In, Garbage Out" crisis.  
* **Trait 2: Participant Continuity:**  Unlike project-based legacy tools, Methodic links qualitative data to the same participant across cycles (e.g., 2024 interview to 2026 survey), allowing for longitudinal tracking.
