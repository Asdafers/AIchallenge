Static Survey Baseline

Traditional win-loss programs rely on static post-sale surveys sent weeks after deal close. Response rates average 15–20%, suffer from recall bias, and surface only surface-level reasons. Sales managers manually code open-text responses — a process taking days per cohort. Coverage of nuanced signals like procurement friction, security objections, and competitor positioning is near zero.
